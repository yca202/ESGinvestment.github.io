About Us

ESG Investing Academy was founded in 2021 with a mission to democratize sustainable investing. We believe that everyone should have access to the knowledge and tools to invest in a better future.

Our team of experienced financial professionals and passionate environmentalists works tirelessly to provide top-quality, actionable education in ESG investing. Through our comprehensive courses, we aim to empower individuals to make informed investment decisions that align with their values.

We're proud to have guided thousands of students from over 50 countries in their ESG investing journey. Join us today and become a part of the movement towards responsible investing.
