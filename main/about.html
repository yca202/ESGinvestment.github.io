About Us

Our Purpose

The growth in investing among students, combined with growing ESG concerns, has presented many with a dilemma that pits their financial interests against their values.

"Like other students, I actively invest, but I also consider myself an environmentally conscious person, and I am aware that these two things can conflict." -Second year undergraduate student.

As a solution to this dilemma, we have created a website that equips students with the necessary knowledge about ESG investing.

However, there is a strong case for optimism. We know there is an abundance of investment opportunities that are not only financially rewarding but also positively influence environmental, social, and corporate governance (ESG) factors. The key lies in gaining the knowledge and skills to identify these opportunities. It is our collective responsibility to stimulate this transformation in investing.

We are a group of students who believe that raising awareness and sharing knowledge about ESG investing can empower other students to address sustainability issues.
